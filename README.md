# Civilisation Simulator

## About
A game where the player develops a society from scratch. Manage your nation well, and be careful not to anger your citizens so as to prevent assassination. Upon death, the game ends.

## Motivation to create
I created this game to merge my peculiar interests of both programming in general and politics to create this simulator. I wanted to provide players the freedom to experiment with whichever economic and social system they desire, allowing them to gain knowledge on various humanities fields such as economics, sociology, history, politics, etc, and explore the interactions between these different subjects. At the same time, I intentionally strayed away from the norms of the traditional strategy games of this genre, most notably by introducing a sense of mortality as the player. This is because of how in Civilisation Simulator, the player is not a god-like figure as with most games, but rather a human which can be killed and replaced if he is not careful enough, paralleling that of the real world where politicians can be ousted.
